About Us

Financial Inventory is a student-driven initiative built to empower individuals with tools for better financial management. Founded in 2025, our mission is to provide simple, accessible, and effective solutions for personal finance, especially for students and young professionals in Bangladesh.

Our Mission

To help people track, manage, and grow their finances with transparency, simplicity, and control.

What We Do

  • Track daily expenses and categorize spending
  • Set monthly budgets and monitor progress
  • Receive personalized financial insights
  • Plan investments based on goals
